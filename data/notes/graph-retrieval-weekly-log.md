## Planning evidence

Mixed-source retrieval performed better than note-only retrieval on planning prompts when the notes did not include implementation details. The clearest gain came from combining note evidence with code-summary evidence for questions about the next experiment.

## Current blocker

The main blocker is still the planning rubric. Until that rubric is explicit, it is difficult to compare note-only retrieval against mixed-source retrieval in a way that feels defensible.
