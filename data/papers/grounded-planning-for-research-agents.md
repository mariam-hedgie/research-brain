title: Grounded Planning for Research Agents
date: 2026-04-10

This paper argues that durable project memory should stay separate from short-horizon planning context. The practical implication for Research Brain is that canonical memory and episodic memory should inform next-step reasoning, while retrieved task context should stay narrow and recent.

The paper is most relevant when deciding why a recommended next step follows from prior work instead of producing a generic planning answer.
